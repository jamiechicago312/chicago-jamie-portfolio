Summary

I'm Jamie and this is a place where you can explore my portfolio. Starting in Nov. 2022, I lead developer relations at Reservoir. Before pivoting to web3 full time, I taught music in public education across the US for 9 years. After completing a WebDev certificate at City College in 2021, I discovered web3 and blockchain technology from a college connection. The rest is hsitory. My typically tech stack consists of Webpack, React, and JavaScript though I have experience in Solidity, Hardhat, and Ganache.

When I'm not working on docs or guides, I'm an active Chicago City Lead for SheFi planning professional events for women and nonbinary people interested in emerging technologies. I also am an active Boys Club member and Farcaster poster.
