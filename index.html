Summary

I'm Jamie and this is a place where you can explore my portfolio. Starting in Nov. 2022, I lead developer relations at Reservoir. Before pivoting to web3 full time, I taught music in public education across the US including orchestra, band, and guitar for 9 years. After completing a web dev certificate at Ctiy College in 2021, I discovered web3 on blockchain technology from a college connection & promptly dove head first into the deep end. My typically tech stack consists of Webpack, React, and JavaScript though I have experience in Solidity, Hardhat, and Ganache.

When I'm not working on docs or guides, I'm an active Chicago City Lead for SheFi planning professional events for women and nonbinary people interested in emerging technologies. I also am an active Boys Club member and Farcaster poster.
